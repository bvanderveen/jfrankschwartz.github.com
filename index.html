Hello!

My name is Frank, and after a long path I have arrived at bodywork as my profession and passion!

My approach to bodywork is holistic, regarding the body-mind as a whole unit and addressing each person where they are at that day. My work will benefit you whether you are in an office or are an athlete! I also have referrals for practitioners of other disciplines for complementary care.

My studio is located on the Woodlawn Triangle across the street from Firehouse restaurant. It is comfortable, spacious and quiet and there is usually ample street parking. I have no set hours, so please call or email to inquire about an appointment. Same day appointment inquiries are welcome. I hope to hear from you!

My education was at East-West College of the Healing Arts. I graduated from their 1002 Hour program (the most in-depth training offered) with a 3.87 GPA.

Under their auspice I studied modalities Cranio-Sacral & Myofascial, Deep Tissue, Sports Massage, Swedish, Thai, Trigger Point and Tui-Na. My instructors included Cyr Foote, Jon Hart, Lisa VanAlstine, James Spake, Allie Longstreth-Lurie, Alex Susbauer and Uma Tupper, all accomplished therapists in their respective disciplines.

I continued my education in Thai massage under Allie Longstretch-Lurie this year with a Level II class, and am planning to travel to Thailand in 2011 for advanced Northern-style training.

Relaxation

I provide a safe space for relaxation massage. My table is heated to keep you cozy and melt stress away while I soothe your body!

Corrective

I also practice structural balancing work, incorporating all of the modalities I have studied. This is to help mitigate or reverse the effects of repetitive patterns that cause daily pain or stiffness!

Thai

Thai massage contains lots of stretching and leaves one feeling energized and invigorated. I can think of nothing better to get your chi flowing!

Couples

I also provide tandem couples' massage. With enough notice, I can offer myself plus another male or female LMT to massage you and your partner at the same time! Or if you prefer, you can each relax on your own tables while I take turns working on both of you.
